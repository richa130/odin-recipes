Balsamic Bruschetta!

Description

When summertime gives you perfectly ripe and juicy tomatoes, one of the best ways to enjoy them is also one of the easiest. Simply dice the tomatoes, season them with a touch of salt, add a generous amount of good quality olive oil, and spoon the mixture over thick slices of toasted or grilled bread. Congratulations, you've just made bruschetta. This 5-star recipe for Balsamic Bruschetta shows you how to bump up that basic tomato mixture with basil, balsamic vinegar, garlic, and Parmesan cheese; and how to toast the bread in the oven â€” a good alternative if you don't have a grill or the weather isn't grill-friendly.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Tomatoes
  • Basil
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Balsamic vinegar

Steps

  1. Spread butter on each piece of bread
  2. Toast bread until golden brown
  3. Toss together tomatoes, basil, cheese, and garlic
  4. Spoon tomato mixture onto bread and serve immediately
